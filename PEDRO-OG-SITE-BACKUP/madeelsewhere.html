Made Elsewhere
1998-1999

C-prints
Each print in Suite of 20 slightly
different size. Average size 18 x 24 in
Suite of 20 Prints, Edition of 10

This work consists of extreme photographic close-ups of various Statue of Liberty figurines, all made outside of New York and the U.S., the large majority made in China. The cropping and composition of all photographs in the set is consistent with the most common tourist postcard of the Statue of Liberty's face in three quarters.

An alternate edition brings all 20 works together in a standard postcard accordion publication.
